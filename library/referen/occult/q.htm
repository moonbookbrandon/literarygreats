Quirardelli, Corneille : A Franciscan born at Boulogne towards the end of the sixteenth century. He studied astrology, and was the author of several astrological and other works.

 

Quirinus, or Quirus : is described as "a juggling stone, found in the nest of the hoopoo." If laid on the breast of one sleeping, it forces him to discover his rogueries.
